Our Story

Crafting Culinary Excellence Through Simplicity

Founded on the principles of seasonality and restraint, Chef Elena Voce brings a modern Mediterranean vision to the heart of the city. We believe that true luxury lies in the quality of raw ingredients and the precision of their preparation.

Every plate at Olea is a canvas of culinary heritage, reimagined for the contemporary palate. We source exclusively from local regenerative farms and artisanal producers.
